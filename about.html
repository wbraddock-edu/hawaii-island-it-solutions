About Island IT

// Founded in Honolulu by local tech pros who believe technology should work for everyone.

// our story

Born in Honolulu. Built for Hawaii.

Island IT Solutions was founded in 2016 by Kainoa Reyes and Marcus Chen, two Honolulu natives who got tired of watching friends and family overpay for mainland IT companies that didn't understand island life. Internet infrastructure, humidity effects on hardware, and the unique needs of Honolulu's small business community — these are things you only learn by living here.

We started with a single truck and two laptops. Today, we have a full shop on S King Street and a team of seven certified technicians. We serve hundreds of home users and dozens of businesses across Oahu.

Our philosophy is simple: treat every client like ohana. That means honest pricing, clear communication, and work we're proud to stand behind.
